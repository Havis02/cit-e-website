We are a tight-knit company, focused on furthering the improvement of the environment. The inclusion of a bike hire service throughout multiple different cities around the country, will minimise the use of cars and therefore minimise the amount of pollution in the air. Our little business was first founded back in 2017, with minimal help and advertising we were able to reach this point and we hope you will join us for the rest of our journey!

Throughout the making of our business, our main aim has always been to limit the amount of pollution in the city air. We hope that in the future we will be able to expand into other more densely populated cities to try and help the traffic and pollution problems.
